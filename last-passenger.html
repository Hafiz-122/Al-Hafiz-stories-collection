The Last Passenger

The bus always left Renala Khurd station at 11:55 PM...

The doors hissed shut. The driver looked at me in the mirror. He didn’t say anything. He just smiled.

The bus jerked forward. Streetlights outside started going… backwards. I checked my phone. Time was reversing.

"Where are we going?" I asked.
“Backwards. One year every night.” the driver replied.

Seat 13 had a message: “Reserved for the one who breaks the rule. The Last Passenger.”

Every night the world moved one year back… but I kept my memories.

On the 10th night I saw my mother alive again… I could save her.

The driver said: “One more stop, then you become the driver.”

I understood. The last passenger is the one who chooses to control time… or let it go.

A message appeared: “Don’t take the bus tomorrow. Some things are not meant to be fixed. - Ammi”

The doors opened. Cold air hit my face. The driver gave me the keys…

Moral

"You can’t fix the past by erasing it. Healing begins when you choose to remember, not to rewind."
