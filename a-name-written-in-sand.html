A Name Written in Sand

Original Story — No Copyright, Free To Use

Hamza, a boy from Karachi, came to the beach every summer vacation. Same bench, same camera, same sunset.

One day he noticed a girl, Zainab, standing with her feet in the water. She held a diary.

Every evening she wrote her father's name in the sand. The waves erased it. And she wrote it again.

"Abu, I come every day so you don't forget me."

Hamza slowly became friends with her.

He learned that Zainab's father had died at sea during a storm two years earlier.

Day after day they met by the shore. Hamza helped her write her father's name. Zainab posed for his camera.

What began as friendship quietly turned into love.

One evening Hamza wrote:

"Zainab + Hamza"

on the sand.

He smiled and said:

"The waves will erase both names. But I'll come every day and write them again."

Zainab laughed softly.

"Promises aren't made in sand, Hamza... they're made in the heart."

The Twist

At the end of that year, Zainab had to move to Canada with her mother.

Her mother had found a better job there, and it was the best opportunity for Zainab's future.

Their farewell felt heavier than the ocean itself.

On their final evening together, Zainab wrote:

"Hamza + Zainab"

Then she looked at him and said:

"Don't write your name anymore, Hamza. Every name written in sand gets erased. Write me in your heart... the waves don't reach there."

The next morning she left.

But Hamza never stopped coming.

Every evening he sat on the same bench. Every evening he wrote one name:

"Zainab"

The waves erased it. He wrote it again.

Happy Ending — 5 Years Later

Five years passed.

The same camera. The same bench. The same sunset.

And the same name in the sand.

Then one evening a familiar voice spoke behind him:

"Attendance is marked, Hamza... you're late."

Hamza froze.

He turned around.

It was Zainab.

The same smile. The same eyes.

Beside her stood a little boy.

Zainab smiled and said:

"This is Hamza Jr... named after you."

Hamza's eyes filled with tears.

He knelt down and wrote:

"Zainab + Hamza + Hamza Jr."

A wave rushed toward the names.

But this time Hamza lifted the child into his arms and protected the writing.

Zainab held his hand and whispered:

"See? A name written in sand may be erased... but a name written in the heart never is."

From that day, the bench was never empty again.

Now three people watched the sunset together.

And Hamza's camera focused on only one thing:

Zainab's smile ❤️🌊

Moral

True love and true waiting never go to waste. What is written in the heart cannot be erased by time.
